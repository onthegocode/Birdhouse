Birdhouse Interiors is a full-service interior design firm specializing in residential and small-scale commercial projects. Anna, the creative force behind Birdhouse Interiors, is passionate about transforming spaces into reflections of her clients' lifestyles and aspirations.  With years of experience in high-end residential projects (link to work page), Anna brings a meticulous eye for detail and a commitment to exceeding expectations.
