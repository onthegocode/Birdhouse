About Birdhouse

Birdhouse Interiors is a full-service interior design firm specializing in residential and small-scale commercial design projects. Anna, the visionary behind Birdhouse Interiors, believes that interior design is about creating an attractive space and enhancing how people live, work, and play. With this philosophy in mind, Birdhouse Interiors approaches every project holistically, considering the space's architecture, environment, and surrounding context. Every project is unique and deserves a personalized approach that delivers exceptional results.  

Meet Anna

As the creative force behind Birdhouse Interiors, Anna brings a passion for transforming spaces into personalized reflections of her client's lifestyles and aspirations. Her journey into interior design began at Meredith College, where she earned a Bachelor of Science in Interior Design. Building on this foundation, Anna pursued further education, graduating with a Master of Business Administration, enriching her design approach with strategic insight and a deep understanding of project management. With years of experience specializing in high-end residential projects, Anna brings a meticulous eye for detail and a commitment to creating environments that exceed expectations. 

The Approach

Every project begins with a commitment to understanding your unique vision. Great design is visually stunning, functional, and tailored to your needs. Whether you are seeking a serene retreat, a vibrant work environment, or a welcoming home, Birdhouse Interiors will collaborate closely with you to translate your ideas into reality. 

Design Philosophy 

At Birdhouse Interiors, we blend creativity with practicality, drawing inspiration from diverse sources to craft spaces that are both timeless and current. The power of thoughtful details and quality craftsmanship in creating harmonious, stylish spaces is paramount.
